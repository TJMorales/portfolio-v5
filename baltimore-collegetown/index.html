Baltimore Collegetown

A unified experience with distinct spaces for different audiences, infused with Baltimore's unique energy.

Clientbaltimorecollegetown.org ↗
RoleUX Design
Agencyidfive
The client

Baltimore Collegetown connects students, colleges, and the community through projects that attract, engage, and retain talent while raising Baltimore's profile as a college destination. It is also a major transportation hub, carrying the transit map and schedule that connect colleges to the city.

The problem

Scattered info, unclear audiences. The site's main audiences were undefined, and visitors could not tell what it offered from its navigation and information architecture. Critical resources, like shuttle information, were spread across the site.

How might we create a unified experience that improves student recruitment, employer connections, and community engagement across the whole network?
The work

Audiences

Visitors were confused about who the site was for. I reworked the information architecture to give each major demographic a distinct space, with audience-based navigation leading the way.

Shuttle

Shuttle information was scattered and hard to parse. I consolidated everything into one cohesive transit page and gave it prominence from the homepage and the architecture, so riders find it fast.

Information hub

College pages were sparse. I folded city-wide amenities into the college templates, creating customizable pages that highlight campus offerings alongside the best things to do nearby.

Impact

The redesign unified a fragmented experience into a clear, audience-focused ecosystem. Restructuring around key user groups, centralizing transit, and elevating college content helped students, families, and employers understand what Collegetown offers, boosting navigation efficiency, shuttle usage, and institutional visibility.

56%Decrease in homepage exits from clearer pathways
52%Faster path to content with audience-based IA
